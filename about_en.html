Dalelk

Dalelk is a website that aims to provide comprehensive, useful information about tourist and cultural events in Syria. We provide an easy-to-use platform to help users discover a variety of events taking place across Syrian cities and regions, including concerts, art exhibitions, cultural festivals, and recreational activities.

We believe in promoting domestic tourism and encouraging people to explore their country's beauty and heritage. Through Dalelk, we make it easier to access event information so users can plan visits and participate in activities they care about.

We update information regularly to ensure accuracy and reliability. We're committed to delivering an excellent user experience with an attractive and easy-to-navigate interface.

Event Submission & Publishing Guidelines

  • Events should be cultural, touristic, family, sports, or artistic in nature.
  • Provide accurate information (title, date, venue, category).
  • The site administration reserves the right to review, edit, or reject submissions that do not comply with guidelines.
  • Events should be public or contribute positively to the local community.
  • Events with content that violates laws or social values are prohibited.
To submit a new event, please contact us via the Contact page.
